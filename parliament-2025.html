As students, we often hear that we are the future ‚Äî but it feels different when leaders sit down to listen to our solutions now.

I had the chance to pitch "RoboServe," my robot waiter prototype that features autonomous navigation and built-in sanitation systems. We also presented Stan's Technology's bioremediation research on treating galamsey-polluted water and a new Solar Tracking System.

Hon. Adomako was impressed with our innovations ‚Äî he didn't just listen, he challenged us.
