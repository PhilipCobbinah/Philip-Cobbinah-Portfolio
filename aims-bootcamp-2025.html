Our Winning Journey

Thrilled to share that our team emerged victorious in the recent AIMS Ghana boot camp! After an intensive week of learning and collaboration, we were tasked with developing innovative solutions in agriculture, health, and climate.

My team and I rose to the challenge, creating a cutting-edge machine learning module that leverages IoT sensors and computer vision to detect early diseases in poultry farms.

Our Solution Includes:

  • User-friendly mobile app for farmers to receive real-time disease alerts
  • Platform bridging investors, farmers, customers, and veterinarians
  • Integration with industry stakeholders like MOFA ðŸ‡¬ðŸ‡­
  • Valuable data analytics for government agencies
  • Early disease detection using computer vision AI

With Ghana's poultry industry contributing over $1.2 billion to the economy and supporting 1.5 million jobs, we aimed to reduce reliance on imports and boost our local sector.

The judges were impressed not only with our idea but also with its potential to provide valuable data for government agencies. We were proud to take home 1st place and a GHC 20,000 award out of 7 competing teams!
