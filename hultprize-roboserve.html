Team Roboserve Wins Hult Prize Campus Competition!

About the Hult Prize

The Hult Prize is a prestigious global social entrepreneurship competition that challenges students to develop innovative solutions to pressing global issues. This year's challenge focused on "Rethinking Food" - and Team Roboserve rose to the occasion!

Congratulations to the Regent University College of Science and Technology team "Roboserve" on winning the Hult Prize Campus competition! Our innovative robot, designed to deliver food in restaurants, impressed the judges with its creativity and potential for impact.

Roboserve Features:

  • Autonomous food delivery in restaurants
  • Coffee mixing capabilities
  • Tissue and hand sanitizer dispensing
  • Phone charging station
  • Smartphone-controlled (with plans for full autonomy)

What's Next?

As campus winners, Team Roboserve will represent Regent University at the Hult Prize National Competition, competing against other top teams from across the country!
