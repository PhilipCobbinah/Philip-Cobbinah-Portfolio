Regent University Student Philip Cobbinah Earns Coveted Spot at Enactus World Cup 2025 in Bangkok

In a remarkable feat of academic excellence and innovation, I was selected to represent Ghana at the 2025 Enactus World Cup. The prestigious global competition took place from September 25-28 at the Queen Sirikit National Convention Center in Bangkok, Thailand, bringing together thousands of students, academics, and business leaders from more than 30 countries.

Our team's success was made possible through strong industry-academic collaboration, particularly the mentorship of Electropro Allied Engineers Ghana, who guided us with expertise in engineering feasibility, sustainable business models, project management, and technical prototyping.
